Card Matching Game

  • It is a memory based card matching game.
  • There are three levels of difficulty easy, medium and hard.
  • You get 3(easy), 5(medium) or 8(hard) seconds to see the tiles before they are hidden.
  • You must choose a diffculty level in the beginning before you can play.
  • In easy mode you have 16 tiles and 120 seconds (2 minutes) to complete the game.
  • In medium mode you have 20 tiles and 150 seconds (2.5 minutes) to complete the game.
  • In hard mode you have 24 tiles and 180 seconds (3 minutes) to complete the game.
  • After selecting the difficulty level you can click on the Start Game to begin.
  • Restart allows you to reset and begin anytime.
  • All the best
